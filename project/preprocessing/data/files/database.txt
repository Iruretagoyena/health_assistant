Below are three tailored diet plans for a 29-year-old, 6'4'', 185-pound man who exercises 3-4 times a week with activities like swimming and strength training. These diet plans are optimized for maintaining muscle mass, providing energy for physical activity, and overall wellness.

1. Vegan Diet Plan
Breakfast: Smoothie with almond milk, banana, spinach, 2 tbsp of peanut butter, 1 scoop of plant-based protein powder, chia seeds.
Mid-Morning Snack: Hummus with baby carrots and cucumber slices.
Lunch: Lentil and quinoa salad with roasted vegetables, avocado slices, and a tahini dressing.
Afternoon Snack: Apple slices with almond butter.
Dinner: Chickpea and sweet potato curry served over brown rice or cauliflower rice.
Dessert: Dairy-free yogurt topped with blueberries and flaxseeds.
Hydration: Water and herbal teas; optional electrolyte drink post-workout.
2. Vegetarian Diet Plan
Breakfast: Greek yogurt parfait with granola, strawberries, and a drizzle of honey.
Mid-Morning Snack: A handful of mixed nuts and a small orange.
Lunch: Spinach and chickpea salad with feta cheese, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and a lemon-olive oil dressing.
Afternoon Snack: Protein smoothie made with oat milk, frozen berries, and whey protein isolate.
Dinner: Mushroom and spinach frittata with a side of roasted sweet potatoes and steamed broccoli.
Dessert: Dark chocolate (70% cocoa) and a banana.
Hydration: Water, with green tea as an optional mid-day beverage.
3. Omnivore Diet Plan
Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and smoked salmon on whole-grain toast.
Mid-Morning Snack: A banana and a boiled egg.
Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, avocados, and a balsamic vinaigrette.
Afternoon Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple chunks.
Dinner: Baked salmon fillet with a quinoa and vegetable medley, including zucchini, bell peppers, and asparagus.
Dessert: Greek yogurt with a sprinkle of walnuts and a drizzle of maple syrup.
Hydration: Water and coconut water post-workout for hydration.
These plans are designed to ensure optimal protein intake, nutrient density, and energy. Each meal plan is balanced with lean protein, complex carbohydrates, healthy fats, and ample fiber to support muscle recovery and maintain energy levels.
